Amazon Price Checker

Hands on: computing prices for Amazon

Create a web page: "Amazon Price Generator" Use prompt( )to ask a few questions: What is the name of the item? Base price? (before markup/markdown) Is today "Black Friday"? Did the purchaser find the product through a search engine? Did purchaser visit a comparison-shopping site? Add logic & concatenate strings to alert "The base price for [item] is $[basePrice]. We will increase the price by 1% to pay the search engine. The purchaser shopped other sites, so we will reduce the price by 10% to get their business. We will reduce the price by 25% since it's Black Friday. The final price is $[finalPrice]."
